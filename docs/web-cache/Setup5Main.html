The inverter will now try to connect to the internet. Your laptop or phone will lose its connection to the inverter's network. As soon as you successfully reconnected to the internet, you will be redirected to the online Solar Monitor. If you're already online and the page does not appear,proceed by clicking here

If the WiFi LED is still blinking after 2 minutes, something went wrong. Did you enter the correct password? To rerun the setup wizard, press the button repeatedly until the WiFi LED blinks 1 time. Reconnect to the Soladin network first and then type http://10.0.0.1 in your browser.

As your inverter tries to connect to the internet, it closes its access point. Therefore, these configuration pages are no longer available. The blinking pattern of the light on the inverter indicates what's happening.
